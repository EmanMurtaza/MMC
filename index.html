What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

Delivering high-quality, affordable healthcare with a focus on the needs of the local Bahria Town community.

General Medicine

Expert consultation for general health concerns, routine checkups, and management of common illnesses.

Diabetes Center

Dedicated diabetes management — monitoring, education, and long-term treatment planning with specialist care.

Gastroenterology

Specialised diagnosis and treatment for gastrointestinal conditions from acid reflux to complex bowel disorders.

Radiology & Diagnostics

Imaging including Ultrasound, X-Ray, CT Scan and MRI — all available within the local community.

Psychology & Counselling

Clinical psychology and behavioural science for mental health support and wellbeing assessment.

Pharmacy & Follow-up

On-site pharmacy support and structured follow-up care to ensure continuity of treatment for all patients.
